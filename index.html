Our Story

Where We Came From

RoboRaptors started as a community-based team built from a basement in New Jersey. We competed in FLL for our first five years, qualifying for the World Championship twice as an FLL team. In our 3rd year as an FTC team, we've grown from a 5-person squad to 9 members — adding four new members over the last three seasons.


Our team goal is to inspire and motivate others in STEM through hands-on engagement, while consistently modeling gracious professionalism and effective collaboration. Everything we build — from our robot to our outreach programs — is designed to create a meaningful impact in our community and beyond.
